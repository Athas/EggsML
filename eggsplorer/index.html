Discoveries

We can use the visualization to determine, which lunch items that are the most popular, and which items we should try to buy more of. As the current data filters wares that are seldomly requested, we can conclude that this lunch club does not like their vegetables. Of all regular items, tomatoes, sweet tomatoes, pickles and onions are the only vegetables. Note, that red onions are bigger and therefore more popular than regular white onions.

The lunch club does not eat white bread. Ryebread is by far the biggest item in the visualization. For all other bread needs, there are primarily Digestive- and TUC biscuits.
